Neymar da Silva Santos Junior

Neymar was born in 1992 on February 5, in Brazil (28 years old). Neymar Jr is a brazilian footballplayer who plays for the French club Paris Saint Germain. Neymar plays with jersey number 10 in Paris Saint Germain and Brazil. He plays as a left winger and an offensive midfielder. Neymar made his debut for the Brazilian national team early on and his efforts on the football field led to great interest from several European clubs, including FC Barcelona, Chelsea FC, Manchester City, FC Bayern and Real Madrid, but in the end Neymar chose to sign a contract with Barcelona.

Football career

Neymar made his debut on March 7, 2009 for Santos in a 2-1 win against Oeste where he played the last 30 minutes. Neymar just turned 17 years old.

In the spring of 2013, Neymar signed for FC Barcelona, despite saying that he did not want to leave Santos until the 2014 World Cup. At his first El Clasico between Barcelona and Real Madrid at the Camp Nou on October 26, 2013, Neymar scored the first goal of the match when Barcelona won 2-1. He also assisted Alexis Sanchez in the second goal. On Thursday, August 3, 2017, Barcelona confirmed that Neymar's contract had been terminated.

On 3 August 2017, Paris Saint-Germain announced that Neymar had signed a five-year contract with the club, which extends to 30 June 2022. His buyout clause and also transition cost EUR 222 million euro. He has become the most expensive transfer in the history of football.
